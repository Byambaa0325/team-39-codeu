Welcome to the Hikers' Hub

Our website is a hub for hikers, like you, around the world. Specifically, we are interested in connecting hikers to form open communities. Our users are able to navigate anywhere on the map to see their country of interest. Each country has a forum, containing all the trails posted by the users, and user discussions.



Why? Each year, more and more people go, and although there are no formal figures, it’s estimated that [Only considering US] National Park usage increases each year and that these days probably some 35 million people go hiking each year in US's 14 National Parks alone. Of those, around 10 million go regularly – at least once a month. In addition, hiking is one of the easiest activity that can be done anywhere.

By using our website, you can navigate to your place of interest and see the trails as markers. You will be able to easily get an oversight of local destinations and hiking spots. Moreover, on clicking the country name, you will be presented with a forum of posts and trails. If you want to know what local hikers are up to, you can join the chatroom and instantly connect.

It all starts with you

Please consider sharing your favourite trails with others. Not only does it make you a star in your local community, it will help hikers who are travelling. All the trails you find here are shared by other hikers just like you who decided to share.
